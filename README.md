## NSF New Hampshire AI Ready Coordination Hub

The **NH AI Ready Coordination Hub** is a statewide digital platform designed to support the National Science Foundation (NSF) AI Ready America initiative.

This portal will serve as the central coordination platform for AI education, workforce development, technical assistance, statewide partnerships, events, resources, and reporting.

---

# Vision

Create the premier statewide AI coordination platform that connects

- Businesses
- Higher Education
- K-12 Education
- Libraries
- Nonprofits
- Workforce Organizations
- Government Agencies
- Community Partners

through responsible Artificial Intelligence adoption.

---

# Project Goals

- Build a modern public website
- Connect organizations across New Hampshire
- Provide AI Readiness Assessments
- Offer Technical Assistance
- Host statewide events
- Maintain a searchable Resource Library
- Collect NSF performance metrics
- Produce real-time dashboards
- Support long-term sustainability

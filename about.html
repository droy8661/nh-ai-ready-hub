About the NH AI Ready Coordination Hub

The NH AI Ready Coordination Hub is a statewide digital platform supporting NSF's AI Ready America initiative in New Hampshire.

Mission

To connect organizations across New Hampshire with AI education, technical assistance, resources, and data-driven coordination.

Vision

Create a scalable, accessible platform that helps businesses, higher education, K-12, libraries, nonprofits, workforce organizations, and government agencies adopt AI responsibly.

Goals

  • Build a modern public website
  • Connect statewide partners
  • Provide readiness assessments and dashboards
  • Support long-term sustainability
